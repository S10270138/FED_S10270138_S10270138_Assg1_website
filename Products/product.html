Our Delicious Bakery Selections

At Duke Bakery, we pride ourselves on crafting the finest artisanal baked goods. From freshly baked bread to delicate pastries and custom cakes, every item is made with care using the best ingredients. Whether you're in the mood for a warm croissant, a soft loaf of bread, or a sweet treat, our bakery has something for everyone.

Our selection of pastries includes buttery croissants, fruity tarts, and melt-in-your-mouth cakes, all made daily to ensure freshness. Each pastry is handcrafted with love and attention to detail, making them perfect for breakfast, tea time, or any moment you want to treat yourself.

Explore our variety of breads, including sourdough, whole grain, and rustic French loaves, all baked to perfection with a golden crust and soft interior. Perfect for sandwiches, toasts, or enjoying with your favorite spreads.
